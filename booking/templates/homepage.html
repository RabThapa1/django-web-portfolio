About Us

Dairy Flat Airport is a regional airport located 30 km north of Auckland’s CBD, just a 25-minute drive via the Northern Motorway. Conveniently positioned near Albany and Silverdale, it offers easy access for travelers on the North Shore.

Established in the late 1950s, the airport has since grown into one of New Zealand’s busiest general aviation hubs, supporting both commercial and private operations.

Airlines & Destinations

Dairy Flat Airport is served by a mix of scheduled and charter airlines, providing regular connections to destinations across New Zealand and internationally. All flights operate from the main terminal, conveniently located at the heart of the airport.
